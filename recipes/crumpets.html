Crumpets

Description

Crumpets, a quintessential British delight, are soft, airy griddle cakes with signature holes on top. Toasted to golden perfection, they boast a delightful contrast of crispy edges and a fluffy interior. Enjoyed with butter or jams, crumpets offer a comforting and satisfying teatime treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Butter or oil for greasing
  • Additional butter for serving

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Activate yeast in warm water and add to flour mixture.
  3. Let batter rise for 1 hour.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in water, fold into batter.
  5. Cook batter in greased rings on a griddle.
  6. Flip and cook until golden and hole-filled.
  7. Toast and serve warm with toppings. Enjoy!
