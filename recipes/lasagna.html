Lasagna

Description

Delight in layers of rich flavors with our classic lasagna recipe. Thin pasta sheets interlace with savory ground beef, velvety béchamel sauce, and tangy marinara, all topped with a golden blanket of melted mozzarella. A comfort food masterpiece that's sure to satisfy every palate.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (9-12 sheets)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or ground beef and pork mixture
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce (white sauce)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook and season ground beef with onion, garlic, tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer
  2. Mix ricotta, Parmesan, and egg for the cheese layer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, then layer: sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce.
  4. Top with béchamel sauce and shredded mozzarella.
  5. Bake covered at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 mins, then uncovered for 15-20 mins until golden.
  6. Rest 10 mins, garnish with parsley, slice, and serve. Enjoy!
